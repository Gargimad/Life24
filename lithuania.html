Lithuania's Orchestra ($70)

On February 10. 2025 at 7pm. Copy paste the brief detail below to your event calendar under the correct date after you've completely filled out this form

The brief detail: Lithuania's Orchestra @7PM

Note that many seats have already been taken. Please choose from the seats available at this moment.

To choose your seat, first pick the side of the gymnasium you want to sit in: A, B, C, or D. Then, you need to pick the row that you'll sit in. The row count starts from the top to the bottom. Separate these characters with a dot. Finally, pick the specific seat in the row based on the direction indicated. For instance, if the direction is to the right, the side is A, and you want to sit in the last row on the last seat, your desired seat will look like this: A.1.36

Please refer to the seating chart at the left to help you choose.

Event Details

Lithuania is an orchestra director who has composed music for almost 30 years now. With all this experience, she is giving us a chance to listen to the beautiful melodies she will direct to her orchestra. Not only is she an expert at orchestral instrument, but her orchestra is also made up of the most talented and experienced players. The beautifully- laid out orchestra is playing her piece, "The little twinkle" on Feb 10. Come bring your family and friends with you to experience this wonderful performance.
